At Koala T-Shirt, we follow ethical principles that reflect professionalism, respect, and accountability. Our Code of Ethics is inspired by industry best practices including ACS and SAGE-AU guidelines.

  • Fair Treatment: We treat all people equally regardless of background or personal characteristics.
  • Privacy: We handle personal information responsibly and in accordance with legal obligations.
  • Communication: We communicate openly, honestly, and clearly with customers and staff.
  • System Integrity: We protect our systems and data from unauthorised access and misuse.
  • Co-operation: We work collaboratively with others to ensure a respectful and productive environment.
  • Honesty: We are transparent about our intentions, capabilities, and actions.
  • Education: We actively maintain and improve our knowledge and skills.
  • Social Responsibility: We consider the impact of our technology and actions on society.
  • Workplace Quality: We maintain a safe, inclusive, and supportive working environment.
